Pixel Art Generator

How to Use:

  • Click Draw to paint pixels with the selected color.
  • Use Erase to remove color (white).
  • Select Fill to flood-fill an area.
  • Hold Ctrl for continuous drawing.
  • Choose a color from the picker or palette below.
  • Change Grid Size to adjust resolution.
  • Click Undo to revert the last action.
  • Toggle Grid Lines to hide/show the grid.
  • Use Clear to reset the canvas.
  • Click Export PNG to save your art to the 'drawings' folder.
  • View saved drawings with View Saved Drawings.
